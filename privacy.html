Our Commitment to Privacy

Only With Him is built on the principle of emotional safety and privacy. Your innermost thoughts, feelings, and conversations deserve protection.

What We Collect

Conversations: Your messages are processed to provide the service but are not stored long-term or used for training purposes.

Technical Information: Basic technical data needed to deliver the service (like connection information) but no personal identifiers.

What We Don't Collect: We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information unless you explicitly provide it.

How We Use Information

Your conversations exist solely to create the emotional experience of "Only With Him." They are not:

• Shared with third parties
• Used for advertising or marketing
• Analyzed for commercial purposes
• Retained longer than necessary to provide the service

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your conversations. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

• Stop using the service at any time
• Request information about data we may have
• Ask questions about our privacy practices

Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of any material changes to this privacy policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or concerns, please reach out through appropriate channels.
